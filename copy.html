Z-Space (Expo Chicago 2023 Partnership)
Materials: Electrical Tape, Incredible artwork from the community

Congruent Space is an immersive [&] interactive fashion boutique from Chicago. A principle component of the experience is art art-based installations and physical environments, with the goal to transport the patrons to an entirely different world, temporarily over-riding the traditional hierarchies within a consumer / producer relationship. When the Miami Design District invited CS to host an extended pop up during the Fall/Winter of 2021-22, 'The Grid' was chosen note only for its wow factor and photogenetic quality, but also because of the implications of a hand applied technique interrupting a high end retail environment.

Of the Worldly Congruent Space. c May 2020.

Of the Worldly
Materials: Custom Fabric, Found trees, Reclaimed Wood, 1x1 Lumber

OF THE WORLDLY for Congruent Space- was an immersive installation throughout an entire retail space presenting a series of narrative installation based environments. The underlying theme an observation of the fashion industry's effect on both our selves and our environment, but the primary goal was to restructure the way we interact with consumer products, refocusing the conversation from the hype or worth of an object to the meaning and intention behind the prices themselves.
'Beauty Supply' Pop Up for Joe Freshgoods. c June 2017.

Beauty Supply for Joe Freshgoods
Materials: Hand-Dyed Sand, (Nautical rope, pulleys, 2 x 4 beams, sourced foliage)

Joe Freshgoods is a contemporary streetwear designer and influencer from Chicago. After numerous high profile brand colabs, JFG wanted to present his new "Stone Island Towel Hoodies" in an artistic light. In launching Joe's solo run, our solution was to present the clothing pieces themselves as art objects in a minimal but impactful environment. 4000 pounds of sand was hand dyed to match the garment color, along with an updated colorway of our Rope n Pulley Hanger System.
